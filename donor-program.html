Donor Program

Egg donation can offer a ray of hope to individuals and couples facing specific fertility challenges. At our Egg Donor Clinic in Faridabad, we extend this option to patients in the following circumstances:

  • Ovarian Dysfunction: When a woman's ovaries fail to produce viable eggs.
  • Ovarian Absence: In cases where the ovaries are absent.
  • Fertilization Difficulties: When eggs obtained during egg retrieval cannot be fertilized by the sperm of proven fertility.
  • Genetic Concerns: When there is a risk of passing on a genetic disorder to the offspring.

Our comprehensive egg donation program involves a series of thorough investigations, expert consultations, and rigorous counseling to ensure a safe and successful journey.

For more information about our Egg Donor Programme, Surrogacy options, IVF treatments, and to take the first step towards your dreams of parenthood, please send your medical and obstetric history reports to our Egg Donation Centre.

We are here to support you on your path to parenthood.

Donor Age

Exclusion criteria-Donors will be excluded if they have a personal or family history of a number of diseases including:

  • Venereal Disease
  • Hepatitis B & C
  • Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome (AIDS)
  • Sickle Cell Trait
  • Thalassaemia Trait

Donors must be in good health and are required to sign a life-style declaration form. Donors and their partners (if applicable) will be screened for Hepatitis B, Hepatitis C, HIV (AIDS) and venereal disease prior to the donation.

Medical Consultation

Potential egg donors have to undergo medical consultations. At first visit the specialist takes a medical history and get blood tests like blood group,vdrl,HIV,hepatitis B antigen,HCV , LH, E2,TSH,AMH and a vaginal ultrasound for antral follicular count,size of the ovaries ,any pathology like ovarian cyst or endometriosis in the ovaries.

At the second visit the test results are reviewed and a decision is made on medical grounds if the potential donor can continue.In case of any discrepancy, a meeting is arranged between the donor & the recipient .The consent forms are signed by both parties.
